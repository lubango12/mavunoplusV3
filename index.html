About Us

At MavunoPlus, we make shopping easy, convenient, and reliable. We deliver fresh fruits, vegetables, dairy, meat, and poultry straight from trusted farms, alongside a wide range of groceries and everyday non-food essentials, all in one order. Our fast and dependable delivery ensures your items reach you safely and on time, while secure digital payment options make checkout simple and worry-free. By connecting local farmers and vendors to customers, we support communities and fair trade, all while embracing eco-friendly delivery practices to reduce waste and protect the environment.

Why Choose Us

There’s a reason why thousands of customers and vendors trust MavunoPlus. We go beyond delivery, we bring convenience, quality, and impact to your doorstep. Every product we deliver is carefully sourced from reliable farmers and suppliers, ensuring freshness and authenticity. Our platform makes ordering simple, payments secure and deliveries fast and eco-friendly. By choosing MavunoPlus, you’re not just getting your essentials quickly. You’re also supporting local farmers, empowering youth, and contributing to a cleaner, more sustainable Tanzania.

Our Partners

MavunoPlus works hand in hand with a network of trusted partners who share our vision of building a more connected, sustainable, and efficient delivery ecosystem in Tanzania. Our partners include smallholder farmers, local producers, wholesalers, retailers, logistics providers, and technology innovators who make it possible to deliver quality food and non-food products to every customer. Through these collaborations, we ensure consistent supply, fair market access, and shared growth that uplifts communities while promoting a greener and more inclusive economy
